# Forward Operating Base

**Coordinated veteran support through operational intelligence**

Forward Operating Base (FOB) is a veteran support platform addressing homelessness and suicide prevention through a six-pillar coordinated response system. We bring military operational discipline to civilian support networks.

## Mission

Connect veterans in crisis to the right resources at the right time through coordinated community partnerships, real-time operational visibility, and dignity-first engagement.

## The Six Pillars

FOB coordinates veteran support across six critical domains:

1. **Housing** - Emergency shelter, transitional housing, permanent supportive housing
2. **Employment** - Job placement, skills training, entrepreneurship support
3. **Healthcare** - VA coordination, mental health services, substance abuse treatment
4. **Legal** - Benefits advocacy, discharge upgrades, legal representation
5. **Financial** - Emergency assistance, financial literacy, debt management
6. **Community** - Peer support, mentorship, veteran service organizations

## Flagship Initiative: Buddy Backup Button

Real-time crisis intervention system connecting veterans to vetted support resources and peer advocates. When a veteran presses the button, the system activates coordinated response across all six pillars based on individual need assessment.

**Core principles:**
- Veteran-controlled activation
- No forced intervention
- Dignity-first engagement
- Coordinated multi-pillar response
- Real-time status visibility for support network

## Platform Architecture

FOB Command Center provides operational visibility and coordination tools for:

- **Partner organizations** - Service providers across the six pillars
- **Mentors** - Peer advocates and veteran supporters
- **Case coordinators** - Professional staff managing veteran engagement
- **Board members** - Leadership oversight and outcome tracking

**Key capabilities:**
- Participant intake and assessment
- Partner pipeline management
- Resource coordination across pillars
- Outcome tracking and reporting
- Grant proposal generation
- Compliance automation

## Technology Approach

Built for operational reliability and regulatory compliance:

- Multi-tenant architecture with organization-level data isolation
- COPPA/FERPA compliance for youth participant protection
- Field-level encryption for sensitive data
- Audit logging for all critical operations
- AI-powered assistance with fail-closed governance controls
- Document-grounded guidance for compliance and procedures

**Tech stack:** Next.js, FastAPI, Firebase, Google Cloud Platform, Anthropic Claude API

## Development Principles

**Security first.** All veteran data treated as regulated. Audit trails, encryption, least privilege access.

**Compliance by default.** COPPA/FERPA controls, PII protection, anonymization requirements built into core architecture.

**Operational discipline.** Every module follows world model principles: explicit state, defined actions, observable transitions, closed feedback loops.

**Dignity-first design.** Veterans control their own data. No forced intervention. Support offered, never imposed.
